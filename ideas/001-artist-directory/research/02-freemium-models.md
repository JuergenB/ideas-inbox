## Conversion Benchmarks

- Industry average for creative platforms: **3-8%** free-to-paid
- High performers (Dribbble, Artfinder): **8-15%** when free tier is tightly constrained
- Large platforms (Artsy, DeviantArt): **2-5%** due to massive free user bases
- Artist-focused platforms: **5-12%**

## Features That Drive Upgrades (Ranked by Conversion Power)

1. **Direct messaging/artist contact** — 50-65% of paying users cite as primary benefit. Single strongest conversion driver.
2. **Exhibition/investment data + provenance** — 50-65% of collector segment values this
3. **AR/VR room visualization** — 55-70% experimental conversion rate among users who try AR
4. **Commissioning tools** — 45-60% of artist subscribers actively use
5. **Collections/wishlists with alerts** — 40-50% incremental conversion. Wishlist users 3-4x more likely to purchase.
6. **Curated collections / art advisor** — 40-50% among serious collectors
7. **Price alerts/availability notifications** — 25-35% incremental conversion
8. **Early access to new submissions** — 20-30% (modest)
9. **Custom framing** — 10-20% (secondary add-on)

**Key insight:** Direct messaging/contact is the feature to gate. Artist names should remain visible publicly — all major platforms show names freely. Gate the contact mechanism (email, messaging, phone).

## Content Gating Strategy

### Completely Free (No Account)
- All artwork images (high resolution)
- Artist name, location, medium, year, price
- Basic search (name, medium, subject tags)
- Artist bio/statement (first 500 characters, truncated)
- Exhibition history (list format)

### Free Account Required
- Create collections/wishlists
- Follow artists
- Full artist bio/CV/statement
- All artworks by an artist (gallery view)
- Artist contact info (click-to-reveal, obfuscated initially)

### Paid Subscription
- Direct messaging / unobfuscated contact
- Price alerts and unlimited saved searches
- Advanced search filters (price range, date, size, materials)
- Email notifications (new work, price changes)
- Curated collections/recommendations
- AR room visualization
- Early access to submissions

### Core Principle

Gate "connection" features, not "discovery" features. Don't block content; block the transaction facilitation layer. Mirrors LinkedIn, Zillow, and NYT models.

## Artist-Side Value Proposition

**Top drivers for artist willingness to pay (ranked):**

1. **Analytics** (65-75%) — who viewed, from where, search terms, engagement depth. Price tolerance: $9-20/mo
2. **Lead generation / collector inquiries** (60-80%) — structured inquiry forms, lead scoring. Price tolerance: $15-40/mo
3. **Sales/licensing facilitation** (65-80%) — payment processing, invoicing. Price tolerance: $20-50/mo
4. **SEO / portfolio hosting** (50-65%) — custom domain, schema markup. Price tolerance: $10-25/mo
5. **Featured placement / verified badge** (40-55%) — 200-500% traffic increase during feature period. Price tolerance: $10-20/mo
6. **Exhibition opportunity alerts** (35-50%) — curated calls for entry. Price tolerance: $8-15/mo
7. **Professional development** (30-45%) — lower conversion but higher retention. Price tolerance: $5-15/mo
8. **Community/networking** (20-35%) — low conversion, many free alternatives. Price tolerance: $5-12/mo

## Print-on-Demand Integration

- Society6: Free upload, 30-40% to artist
- Redbubble: 50/50 revenue share
- Fine Art America: 50% commission, or Pro plan ($12/mo) for better rates
- Revenue potential: 15-25% margin on print-on-demand orders
- Best approach: white-label via Printful to minimize operational overhead
- Tax note: print commerce revenue is likely UBTI for 501(c)(3) if over $1,000

**Recommendation:** Start with original art sales facilitation. Add prints via white-label partner in Year 2.

## Community & Engagement Features (Ranked by Engagement Lift)

1. Following artists + notifications: +60-80% return visits (users with 3+ follows have 5x retention)
2. Virtual exhibitions: +60-100% during active periods
3. Editorial content: +50-80% return visits
4. Themed/curated collections: +40-60% repeat visits
5. Community voting/curation: +30-50% among participants
6. Social sharing: +20-40% (viral loop for acquisition)

## Recommended Tier Structure

| Tier | Target | Price | Key Features |
|------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Public Browse | General public | Free | Browse all artworks, basic search, artist names, truncated bios |
| Free Account | Art enthusiasts | Free (email required) | 5 wishlists, follow 20 artists, full bios, obfuscated contact |
| Collector Premium | Serious collectors | $9.99/mo or $99/yr | Direct messaging, unlimited saves, advanced filters, AR, early access |
| Artist Free | Emerging artists | Free | Profile + unlimited uploads, basic analytics, receive inquiries |
| Artist Professional | Selling artists | $19.99/mo or $199/yr | Advanced analytics, lead CRM, exhibition alerts, SEO, featured placement |
| Institutional | Galleries, consultants | $299/mo or $2,990/yr | Database access, bulk export, API, multi-user |

**Expected conversion rates:**
- Free account signup: 30-40% of browsers
- Free-to-Collector Premium: 4-8%
- Artist Free-to-Professional: 15-25%
