## Subject

Demo: A Marketing Agency in a Box — and What It Means for Vista Growth

## Description

I want to show you something I built: PolyWiz, a system that does what a mid-size social media team does — scrapes content, generates platform-specific posts across 13 channels, schedules them over months, manages brand voice, and publishes with human approval on every post. It's running real campaigns for four arts organizations right now. One person built it.

I think this is relevant to the conversation about Vista Growth in two ways: first, it's a concrete example of the kind of AI-driven structural change you'll be advising clients on. Second, the boilerplate template approach I used to build it is something Jackie could use to start developing Vista Growth's own internal tools — like turning the AI Transformation Audit into an interactive self-service assessment.

## What I'll Cover
